# OUTSINC - Outreach Someone In Need of Change

> *A unified, trauma-informed platform to support people facing homelessness, substance use, mental health, poverty, and related crises.*

## 🎯 Vision

OUTSINC is built on the principle that **technology supports human relationships, not the other way around**. We create systems that:
- Center lived experience in every design decision
- Support "tell your story once" - no re-traumatization
- Enable person-centred, harm reduction, trauma-informed care
- Turn real experience into data that shifts systems

## 🏗️ System Architecture

### Core User Types

The platform serves four primary user groups:

1. **Clients / Peers**
   - Anyone needing support in any area of life
   - Access to their own profile, appointments, consents, resources
   - Self-service tools for managing their journey

2. **Outreach & Case Workers**
   - Direct support staff: outreach, intake, case management, harm reduction
   - Access to client profiles, assessments, notes, tasks
   - Tools for coordination and follow-up

3. **Business / Public**
   - Community members reporting concerns or seeking information
   - Access to public resources, reporting tools, education
   - Safe way to help without causing harm

4. **Partner Agencies**
   - External services (shelters, clinics, legal, etc.)
   - Consent-based information sharing
   - Referral coordination and outcome tracking

## ⚡ Core Principles

**Person-Centred**
- Clients control their information and consent
- Flexible pace and depth of engagement
- "Skip this question" always available

**Trauma-Informed**
- One topic at a time, with breaks
- Clear explanations of why information is needed
- Safe, predictable interactions

**Harm Reduction**
- Non-judgmental supply distribution
- Risk assessment that respects autonomy
- Safety planning with client input

**Lived Experience Leadership**
- Peers drive design and feedback
- Co-governance tools for real power
- Technology supports relationships, never replaces them

Our visual and interaction design is:
- **Empowering, not pity** - Show strength and resilience
- **Welcoming, not clinical** - Warm colors and organic shapes

## 📦 Core Modules (Version 1.0)

### Access & Authentication
Secure, role-based access with trauma-informed recovery options for people who lose phones or forget passwords.

### Client Intake & Registration
Gentle, flexible onboarding that respects pace and allows "quick start" or "deep dive" approaches.

### Consent & Privacy Management
Digital consent that's visible, changeable, and never buried - clients control who sees what.

### Needs Assessment & Smart Surveys
Multi-step questionnaires with conditional logic that adapt to each person's situation.

### Risk Assessment & Safety Planning
Structured risk identification with client-centred safety plans for crisis situations.

### Quality of Life Tracking
Before/after metrics that measure what matters to people, not just bureaucratic outcomes.

### Case Management & Notes
Living record of contacts, goals, and progress - "tell your story once" made real.

### Harm Reduction Ordering & Inventory
Organized supply distribution with favorites, presets, and data-informed restocking.

### Resource Directory & Referral Engine
Searchable services with warm referral tools and outcome tracking.

### Public / Business Reporting
Community concern reporting that leads to support, not displacement.

### Events, News & Community Engagement
Keep everyone informed with impact updates, events, and success stories.

### eLearning & Knowledge Library
Toolkits, guides, and courses for clients, staff, businesses, and community.

### Dashboards & Analytics
Turn experience into evidence for advocacy, funding, and system change.

### Admin & Configuration
Keep the system tidy, secure, and adaptable as needs evolve.

### Client Self-Service Portal
Clients see their progress, manage consents, access resources independently.
